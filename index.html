Why?

We want to pursue this business idea because we are passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems and improve lives, whether by inspiring students through robotics education or enhancing independence for specially-abled individuals.

What?

Our venture, Quest Lab, is dedicated to revolutionizing education and accessibility through innovative robotics solutions. We specialize in two main areas: crafting interactive and educational robotic learning kits tailored for students, and designing advanced robotic arms to assist individuals with disabilities in daily tasks.

How?

Quest Lab solves the problem of limited STEM education engagement and accessibility for individuals with disabilities through innovative robotics solutions. Our revenue is generated through sales of kits to educational institutions and individuals, subscription-based access to our educational platform, direct sales of robotic arms, and partnerships with healthcare providers.
